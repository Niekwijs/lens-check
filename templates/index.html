Lens Check

Lens Check will help you decide what type of lenses you should be buying, and possibly help save you money.

This checker will give you a recommendation based on other people their experience with different type of lenses. At the end we will give a percentage on how good the different type off lenses will possibly fit.

Before making the recommendation we need you to answer some questions about your eyes.
